Behind the Sunrise Story

Who We Are & What We Solve

Sunrise Operations, LLC is a Florida-based support services firm built to make complex day-to-day operations manageable. We coordinate people, paperwork, and providers across administrative and support functions where delays and ambiguity silently drive up costs. Our team integrates with your internal staff, translating organizational goals into practical routines that vendors and crews can actually follow. We map responsibilities, audit data inputs, and establish exception paths so nothing important falls through the cracks. The result is a calmer operation—complete records, predictable service windows, and clear accountability from the back office to the job site.

While we serve clients nationally, our administrative hub is in South Miami. From here we manage intake, scheduling, and service confirmation; coordinate waste handling or remediation partners when needed; and compile performance dashboards for leadership. Our specialty is constructing workflows that scale—simple to adopt, strict on compliance, and flexible enough to adapt to local constraints without losing control.

We focus on outcomes that matter

  • Reduced cycle times from request to verified completion.
  • Cleaner vendor records: licenses, COIs, W-9s, and safety attestations.
  • Lower rework through detailed scopes and photo-based acceptance.
  • Better visibility: weekly exception logs and SLA compliance snapshots.
  • Audit-ready documentation for environmental and remediation tasks.

Clear Steps, Predictable Results

Our Three-Step Operating Rhythm

1) Discovery & Workflow Design

We document current state, stakeholders, and bottlenecks; define acceptance criteria; and convert tribal knowledge into forms, decision trees, and communication cadences. The goal is a lightweight workflow your team actually wants to use.

2) Launch & Vendor Enablement

We onboard providers, verify credentials, and pilot the process on a small set of requests. Early findings are folded back into scopes and templates. Stakeholders receive a simple playbook and escalation path.

3) Run, Measure, Improve

We coordinate daily work, track SLAs, and publish exception logs and trend snapshots. As patterns emerge, we update the workflow—reducing noise and improving time-to-resolution without sacrificing control.
